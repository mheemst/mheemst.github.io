U.S. Health Score Map

This interactive SVG map features the health scores of each state from 2007 to 2017. Each health score is plotted as a blue circle on its corresponding state. By moving the slider or pressing the buttons, the user can watch the circles change size related to change in health score per year. The data is plotted using D3.js. Hovering over the states displays their names in the top right corner.
