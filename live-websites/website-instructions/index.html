How to Submit a Story on Wattpad

Wattpad is a website for authors to submit their stories and for readers to enjoy these stories. To submit a story, an author needs to go through a simple process.

Show or hide steps.












Write the story in another program, like Word or Google Docs.

Go to Wattpad and look at the navigation at the top of the page. Click on the Create button.

Click the New Story button.

Now you will see labeled fields and a gray image to the left that says "Add a cover." First, fill out the fields for title and description.

Next you will see an area to add tags. Click the "Add a tag" button. Type a word or phrase that people can search for in order to find your story. Tags are seperated by spaces, so if you type a phrase, it will either have to be seperated into individual words or typed all together with no spaces.

Select the genre and language that your story is written in.

Select the copyright information that you would like for your story. Most authors choose to reserve all rights. For more information on the options, click the gray circle next to the drop down menu, then click the link that says "Learn more."

Indicate if your story has mature content. For examples of what is considered mature content, click the gray circle next to the field, then click the link that says "Learn more."

Now you will create a cover. Assuming you have an image selected already, it just needs to be resized to fit Wattpad's dimensions. Wattpad covers are 512x800 pixels. Find an art program that will allow you to create a canvas this size, fit your image to it, then save it as a PNG.

Remember to include the title of the story as well as your pen name or username.

In the top right corner of the screen, click the "Next" button. You will now see a page for the first chapter. Copy and past the title and the chapter contents from your other document. Then in the top right corner, click the Publish button.

You have now published your story with its first chapter available.
