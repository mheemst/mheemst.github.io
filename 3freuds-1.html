3 Freuds Brewing Co.

"3 Freuds" is a website for a made up brewry. I worked with two others, hence the "3" in the name. My contribution was the Merch page and Beers page. The Merch page has a jQuery popup window that appears when the user clicks on a product. I made the logo with Adobe Illustrator and Photoshop.
My group was using 3 Floyds Brewing as inspiration, but we wanted our content to be original. So one of my partners said, "Hey... you know what kinda sounds like 'Floyd'?" Several Sigmund Freud puns ensued.
